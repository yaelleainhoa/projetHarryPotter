GODRIC GRYFFINDOR

Godric Gryffindor was an English wizard and one of the four founders of Hogwarts. His birthplace became known as Godric's Hollow. He is the only one who is not mentioned to have any descendant. The symbol of his house is a lion.

He praised courage, determination and strength of heard above all. He wanted his students to be brave and determined. Unlike Slytherin, he accepted muggle born students. He was a good duelist and was also skilled in muggle dueling, unsing his sword in combats. However, according to Goblin, his sword belonged to them since they estimate the sword belongs to its creator.

Fun fact : The founder were unsure of how they would Sort the students when they were dead. To solve this, Gryffindor took off his hat and all four of them enchanted it so that it could sort the students long after their deaths. That's how the Sorting Hat was created!
