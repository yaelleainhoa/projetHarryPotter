HELGA HUFFLEPUFF

HHelga Hufflepuff was a Welsh witch and was one of the four founders of Hogwarts. We don't know much about her descendance, except that one of her relics, a small golden cup, belonged to one of her descendants before Tom Marvolo Riddle seized it. The symbol of her house is a badger.

While the other founders chose to have special students, she accepted them all without preference, but the students of her house are described as loyal and hard-working. She was a woman of tolerance, willing to accept Muggle-born students unlike Slytherin. She also Took in the housse-elves to work in Hogwarts Kitchen, a place were they wouldn't be abused and bullied.

Fun fact: She was highly gifted in food-related charms and many of her recipes are still used in Hogwarts today.
