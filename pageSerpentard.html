SALAZAR SLYTHERIN

Salazar Slytherin was a pure-blood wizard, one of the greatest of the age, and one of the four founders of Hogwarts. He comes from a pure-blood family and has the rare ability of speaking Parseltongue. Tom Marvolo Riddle was one of his descendants and thus also speaks parseltongue. The symbol of his house is a snake.

His students were handpicked for their resourcefulness, determination, and a disregard for the rules. He also thought bloodline was important and didn't even accept muggle born students in Hogwarts. However, the others founders didn't agree. Eventually, Slytherin and Gryffindor argued and even dueled, which led to the farewell of Slytherin. Before leaving Hogwarts, created the Chamber of Secrets, the purpose being to "purge the school of all muggle-born students".

Fun fact : Slytherin created his own hand, made of snakewood and a core of basilisk horn, which has the unique ability to "sleep" when commanded in parseltongue.
